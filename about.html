About Eric

With a rich career spanning three decades, Eric is currently a student in MIT's XPRO Full Stack Development Program, mastering the MERN stack.

Prior to that, he founded and led Blackriver.cc, a multi-tiered digital cycling platform, culminating in a successful exit and acquisition by Trek Bicycle.

Before the entrepreneurship, Eric spent 19 years as Trek's Global Creative Director, establishing an integrated Design Studio, and helping transform Trek into a global consumer brand. His unique background in entrepreneurship, technology, fine arts, leadership, and user experience helps position him as a unique full-stack developer and leader.
